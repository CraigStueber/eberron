Nation Changes

For Eberron

Dwarf

At the beginning of the Last War. The house Kundarak led the united dwarven kingdom away from Khorvaire, to lands unknown. There are still small pockets of dwarves that were left behind, living among humans. The remaining dwarves have kept to themselves, working as hired hands, blacksmiths, and mercenaries.

Warforge

It is common thought that the warforges should have destoried after the last war. There is a private company in Sharn that is paying 1000 gold for your used warforges, so they can be destoried. Warforge populations have been quickly lowering in all towns and cities across Khorvaire.

Gnoll

When the humans tried to 'tame' the mighty nomadic warrior race. What was once a great Gnoll horde that ran through Khorvaire, split into many smaller tribes. One tribe who took the knowledge they had learned from the humans and built great sea ships, and sailed away from Khorvaire.

Changeling

Greatly Untrusted. The changeling race has been hunted and blamed for almost all that is wrong with the world. Most of Khorvaire require changelings to wear a white arm band on their right arms. There is an underground group called the Tyrants that have been leading a small group of changelings in hopes to gain equal rights.
